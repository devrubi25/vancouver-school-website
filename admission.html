    Everything you need to know to join our school

    Eligibility

    • Nursery: 3–5 years
    • Class 1 onward: Previous class pass certificate
    • Inclusive environment for all backgrounds

    Documents Required

    • Aadhaar Card (child)
    • Birth Certificate
    • Transfer Certificate (Class 1+)
    • Passport-size photographs
    • Caste Certificate (if applicable)

    Admission Process

    1. Collect and fill the Admission Form.
    2. Submit all required documents.
    3. Child & Parent Interaction.
    4. Admission confirmation.
    5. Fee payment & orientation details.
